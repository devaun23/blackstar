# Murmur Buzzwords

## Systolic

- **Aortic Stenosis**: SEM cresc/decresc, louder w/ squatting, softer w/ valsalva. + parvus et tardus
- **HOCM**: SEM louder w/ valsalva, softer w/ squatting or handgrip
- **Mitral Valve Prolapse**: Late systolic murmur w/ click, louder w/ valsalva and handgrip, softer w/ squatting
- **Mitral Regurgitation**: Holosystolic murmur radiates to axilla w/ LAE

## Congenital / Continuous

- **VSD**: Holosystolic murmur w/ late diastolic rumble in kiddos
- **PDA**: Continuous machine-like murmur
- **ASD**: Wide fixed and split S2

## Diastolic

- **Mitral Stenosis**: Rumbling diastolic murmur with an opening snap, LAE and A-fib
- **Aortic Regurgitation**: Blowing diastolic murmur with widened pulse pressure and eponym parade
