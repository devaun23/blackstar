# Down Syndrome (Trisomy 21)

- Decreased tone, oblique palpebral fissures, simian crease, big tongue, white spots on iris (Brushfield spots) -> Down Syndrome
- Expected IQ? Moderate mental retardation. Speech, gross and fine motor skill delay.
- Heart? VSD, endocardial cushion defects
- GI? Hirschsprung's, intestinal atresia, imperforate anus, annular pancreas
- Endocrine? Hypothyroidism
- MSK? Atlanto-axial instability
- Neuro? Increased risk of Alzheimer's by 30-35 (APP is on Chr21)
- Cancer? 10x increased risk of ALL

# Edward's Syndrome (Trisomy 18)

- Omphalocele, rocker-bottom feet/hammer toe, microcephaly and clenched hand -> Edward's syndrome (Trisomy 18)

# Patau's Syndrome (Trisomy 13)

- Holoprosencephaly, severe mental retardation and microcephaly, cleft lip/palate -> Patau's syndrome (Trisomy 13)

# Turner Syndrome (XO)

- 14 year old girl with no breast development, short stature and high FSH -> Turner syndrome (XO)
- MC genotype of aborted fetuses
- Associated anomalies? Horseshoe kidney, coarctation of aorta, bicuspid aortic valve
- Tx? Estrogen replacement for secondary sex characteristics and to avoid osteoporosis

# Klinefelter Syndrome (XXY)

- 18 year old tall, lanky boy with mild MR has gynecomastia and hypogonadism -> Klinefelter syndrome
- Increased risk for gonadal malignancy

# Neurofibromatosis

- Cafe-au-lait spots, seizures, large head. Autosomal dominant -> Neurofibromatosis

# Pierre Robin Sequence

- Mandibular hypoplasia, glossoptosis, cleft soft palate -> Pierre Robin Sequence
- Associated w/ FAS or Edwards

# Smith-Magenis Syndrome

- Broad, square face, short stature, self-injurious behavior. Deletion on Chr17 -> Smith-Magenis

# Prader-Willi Syndrome

- Hypotonia, hypogonadism, hyperphagia, skin picking, aggression. Deletion on paternal Chr15 -> Prader-Willi

# Angelman Syndrome

- Seizures, strabismus, sociable w/ episodic laughter. Deletion on maternal Chr15 -> Angelman

# Williams Syndrome

- Elfin-appearance, friendly, increased empathy and verbal reasoning ability. Deletion on Chr7 -> Williams

# Cornelia de Lange Syndrome

- IUGR, hypertonia, distinctive facies, limb malformation, self-injurious behavior, hyperactive -> Cornelia de Lange

# Fetal Alcohol Syndrome

- Microcephaly, smooth philtrum, thin upper lip, ADHD-like behavior -> Fetal Alcohol Syndrome
- Most common cause of mental retardation

# Fragile X Syndrome

- CGG repeats on the X-chr w/ anticipation. Macrocephaly, macro-orchidism, large ears -> Fragile X Syndrome
- Most common type of MR in boys

# Waardenburg Syndrome

- Autosomal dominant, or associated w/ advanced paternal age. Short palpebral fissures, white forelock and deafness -> Waardenburg Syndrome
