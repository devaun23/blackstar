# Bruton Agammaglobulinemia

- 2 y/o M w/ multiple ear infections, diarrheal episodes & pneumonias. No tonsils seen on exam -> Bruton agammaglobulinemia
- X-linked
- Infections start @ 6-9mo (maternal IgG wanes)
- Labs? Absence of B cells on flow cytometry, low levels of all Igs

# Combined Variable Immune Deficiency (CVID)

- 17 y/o F with decreased levels of IgG, IgM, IgE, and IgA but normal numbers of B cells -> CVID (acquired)
- Complication? Increased lymphoid tissue -> increased risk for lymphoma

# Selective IgA Deficiency

- Most common B-cell defect. Recurrent URIs, diarrhea -> Selective IgA deficiency
- Complication? Anaphylaxis reaction if given blood containing IgA

# DiGeorge Syndrome

- 3 wk old M with seizure, truncus arteriosus, micrognathia -> DiGeorge Syndrome
- Genetic defect? Microdeletion on Chr22
- What types of infections in childhood? Candida, viruses, PCP pneumonia

# SCID (Severe Combined Immunodeficiency)

- Infant w/ severe infections, no thymus or tonsils. Severe lymphopenia -> SCID
- See infections w/ bacterial, viral and opportunistic bugs
- Inheritance? MC is XLR. AR is an ADA deficiency
- Tx? Pediatric emergency! Need bone marrow transplant by age 1 or death.

# Chronic Granulomatous Disease (CGD)

- 3 y/o M child w/ recurrent swollen, infected lymph nodes in groin and staph aureus skin abscesses -> CGD
- XLR. PMNs can ingest but not kill catalase-positive bugs
- How to diagnose? Nitroblue tetrazolium test (yellow means they have the disease). New test is flow cytometry w/ DHR-123

# Wiskott-Aldrich Syndrome

- 18 mo M baby w/ severe eczema, petechiae, and recurrent ear infections -> Wiskott-Aldrich Syndrome
- Often present w/ prolonged bleeding after circumcision
- Ig makeup? Low IgM, high IgA and IgE, slightly low IgG
