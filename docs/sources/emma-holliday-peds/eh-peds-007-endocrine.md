# Pediatric Diabetes / DKA

- 12 y/o girl with 2 day history of vomiting. For the last 4 weeks, weight loss, polyphagia, polydipsia and polyuria.
- Na = 130, Cl = 90, HCO3 = 15, glucose = 436

## Acute Management

- Next best step? Start insulin drip + IVF. Monitor BGL and anion gap. Start K. Bridge w/ glargine once tolerating PO.

## Pathophysiology

- T-cell mediated destruction of islet cells
- Insulin autoantibodies, glutamic acid decarboxylase autoantibodies

## Diagnostic Criteria

- Fasting glc >125 (twice)
- 2hr OGTT (75g) >200
- Any glc >200 + symptoms

## Long-term Treatment

- Will need insulin therapy
