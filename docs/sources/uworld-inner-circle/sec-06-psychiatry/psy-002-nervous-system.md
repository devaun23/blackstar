## Cognitive and Developmental Anomalies

### Fragile X Syndrome

- Pathophysiology
  - CGG repeat expansion of FMR1
  - Gene methylation silences FMR protein
  - X-linked Dominant
- Neuropsychiatric features
  - Developmental delay
  - Intellectual disability
  - ADHD
  - Autism Spectrum Disorder
  - Self-injurious behavior (hand biting)
  - Anxiety
- Exam
  - Long face with prominent forehead and chin
  - Large, protruding ears
  - Macroorchidism (>8 years old)
  - Macrocephaly
  - Joint hypermobility (finger, wrists)
- Dx: FMR1 DNA analysis (measure length of CGG repeat)
