# Therapy Types

## Biofeedback

- Indication: Prominent physical symptoms, pain disorders
- Improves control over physiological reactions to emotional stress

## Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

- Indications
  - 1st line for OCD (exposure and response therapy)
  - Depression, GAD, PTSD, Panic Disorder, Eating Disorder
  - Negative thought patterns
- Challenges maladaptive cognitions
- Targets avoidance with behavioral techniques (relaxation, exposure, behavior modification)

## Dialectical Behavioral Therapy (DBT)

- Indication: Borderline Personality Disorder
- Integrates CBT with mindfulness, distress tolerance, and emotional regulation
- Decreases self harm

## Interpersonal Psychotherapy

- Indication: Depression
- Links symptoms to current relationship conflict and interpersonal skills deficit

## Motivational Interviewing

- Indication: Substance use disorders
- Nonjudgmental; acknowledges ambivalence and resistance; enhances intrinsic motivation to change

## Psychodynamic Psychotherapy

- Indications: Higher functioning patients, Personality disorders
- Traces problems back to origin in childhood and unconscious conflict
- Uses transference

## Supportive Psychotherapy

- Indications: Lower functioning patients, Psychotic disorders, Patients in crisis
- Helps reduce stress, improve coping skills and maintain hope
