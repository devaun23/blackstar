# CUTANEOUS MYCOSES

## TINEA CORPORIS

- MC: Trichophyton
- Ring shaped lesion with peripheral scaling
- "Flower petal" shape
- Dx: KOH prep of skin scrapings
- Tx:
  - 1st line/localized: Topical—Clotrimazole, Miconazole, Terbinafine
  - 2nd line/extensive: Oral—Terbinafine, Griseofulvin

## TINEA FACIALIS

- Erythematous annular patch or plaque with light, diffuse scales
- Tx: Oral antifungals due to extensive skin infection

## TINEA MANUUM

- Hands
- Pruritic scaly patches on palms or annular plaques with a raised border on the dorsum
- Concurrent Tinea Pedis via auto inoculation from scratching

## TINEA PEDIS

- Topical Ketoconazole, Terbinafine

## TINEA VERSICOLOR

- Most visible after extensive sun exposure due to tanning of adjacent skin

## ERYTHRASMA

- Armpits, groin, between toes (possible intergluteal folds, submammary, periumbilical skin)
- Well defined pink or brown patches with fine scaling and superficial fissures
