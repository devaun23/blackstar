# Urology / Oncology

## Bladder Cancer

65 yo M with 2 weeks hematuria + 20 lb weight loss. Plumber. PMH EGPA. Non-smoker. UA with hematuria, no dysmorphic RBCs → bladder cancer
RF in this case? Meds (likely cyclophosphamide for EGPA)

Cyclophosphamide AE: hemorrhagic cystitis, bladder cancer
Prevention? Mesna
Dx? Cystoscopy w/ bx

RF for bladder cancer: smoking, Schistosoma haematobium, aniline dyes, cyclophosphamide

## Renal Cell Carcinoma

Flank mass + hematuria + new-onset varicocele in longtime smoker → RCC
Most common type? Clear cell
Dx? Do NOT biopsy
Tx? Nephrectomy
Paraneoplastic syndrome? Polycythemia 2/2 EPO production

# Pharmacology

## ACE-Inhibitor Contraindications

Bilateral renal artery stenosis or already low GFR
- Pathophys: ATII constricts efferent arteriole. ACE-I → low ATII → efferent arteriole dilates → decreased GFR → possible renal failure

Hereditary angioedema
- Pathophys: C1 esterase inhibitor deficiency. Both C1 esterase and ACE break down bradykinin. Block ACE → can't break down bradykinin → angioedema

Pregnancy

# Pulmonology

## PCP Pneumonia

45 yo F Crohn's on etanercept. Dyspnea + nonproductive cough + fever + 12 days malaise. Na 131. PaO2 57. CXR "diffuse ground glass interstitial infiltrates." PPD 3 yrs ago negative. No sick contacts → PCP pneumonia
Many brain or lung pathologies can cause SIADH
Labs? Elevated serum LDH
Dx? BAL w/ silver-stain positive organisms

RF: HIV, TNF inhibitors
Before starting a TNF inhibitor → screen for TB and Hep B

Contrast with Strep pneumo → more rapid onset, severe sxs, lobar consolidation
Contrast with Legionella → pneumonia + diarrhea/abdominal pain + hyponatremia + exposure to water source (hotel, fountain, air conditioning)
Contrast with TB → fever + night sweats + hemoptysis + cavitary lesion (not interstitial infiltrates)


# Endocrinology — Thyroid

## Hashimoto's Thyroiditis

33 yo F with fatigue + loss of interest + 10 lb weight gain + bilateral LE edema + missed last 2 periods → Hashimoto's thyroiditis
Dx? TSH
Ab? Anti-TPO
Histology? Lymphocytic infiltrate of thyroid gland
Associations: other autoimmune diseases (vitiligo, pernicious anemia)

Complications:
- Initial thyrotoxic phase (release of preformed thyroid hormone)
- Hx Hashimoto's + rapidly enlarging thyroid → thyroid lymphoma

Why the missed periods?
Low T3/T4 → TRH release → TSH & prolactin release → prolactin suppresses HPG axis

## Secondary Hypothyroidism

Low TSH + low T3/T4 → secondary hypothyroidism
Causes: craniopharyngioma (derived from Rathke's pouch), Sheehan's syndrome

## De Quervain's Subacute Thyroiditis

Painful thyroid + hx viral URI → De Quervain's
Can present as hypothyroid or hyperthyroid (2/2 release of preformed hormone)
RAIU? Low uptake (not producing thyroid hormone)

## Euthyroid Sick Syndrome

ICU pt + mildly low T3/T4 + mildly low TSH → euthyroid sick syndrome

## TBG & Estrogen

High estrogen → increases TBG → higher total T4 but free T3 normal → clinically euthyroid

## Thyroid Cancer

Labs? Elevated TSH (thyroid cancers tend to be hypofunctional)
Dx? US with biopsy
#1 RF? Radiation to head & neck

Papillary: most common. Spread: lymphatic. Histology: psammoma bodies + Orphan Annie eyes
Follicular: spread hematogenously
Medullary: tumor marker calcitonin → hypocalcemia → prolonged QT
- Association: MEN2A/2B
- Mutation: RET gene (AD inheritance)
- Prevention: prophylactic thyroidectomy (risk = 100%)
- Histology: apple green birefringence on Congo red stain (amyloid)
