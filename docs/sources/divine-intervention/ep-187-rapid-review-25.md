# Immunodeficiency

6 month old w/ CXR showing interstitial infiltrates + BAL with PJP + low IgG, IgE, IgA + high IgM → hyper IgM syndrome
Pathophys: problems with class switching (IgM → IgG & IgA)
Requires CD40-CD40L interaction
PJP in child ddx? SCID, DiGeorge, Hyper IgM

Child with recurrent Staph aureus abscesses → chronic granulomatous disease
Inheritance? X-linked recessive
Pathophys? NADPH oxidase deficiency
Cell involved? Neutrophil
Dx? DHR test, NBT test (older)
Tx? Interferon-gamma & IVIG

## Interferon Uses
Interferon-alpha → hep B and C (MDD is relative CI)
Interferon-beta → MS
Interferon-gamma → CGD
IVIG → Bruton's, Hyper IgM, Guillain-Barre

Newborn w/ hypocalcemic seizures + tetralogy of Fallot → DiGeorge syndrome
Pathophys? 3rd and 4th pharyngeal pouches fail to form → no thymus and no parathyroid glands
Cell involved? T cells
Imaging? No thymic shadow on NB CXR (also seen in SCID)

Child with infections where WBC count is high but no pus + delayed separation of umbilical cord stump → Leukocyte adhesion deficiency
Pathophys? Lack CD-18 or beta-2-integrin → neutrophil cannot adhere to endothelial cells
Inheritance? AR
May also have bleeding (Glanzmann thrombasthenia-like defect)

## Hereditary Angioedema
Pt given enalapril, now c/o severe abdominal pain
Pathophys? C1 esterase inhibitor deficiency → cannot shut down kallikrein pathway → increased bradykinin → edema of lips, airway, GI tract
ACE breaks down bradykinin, so ACE-Is remove this breakdown pathway
Dx? C2 & C4 levels low
Tx? Ecallantide (direct inhibitor of kallikrein), Icatibant (bradykinin receptor antagonist)

# Genetics — Imprinting

## Prader-Willi Syndrome
Child hypotonic at birth + now 15 yo obese + almond-shaped eyes
Chromosome? 15
Maternal gene turned off → maternal uniparental disomy or paternal deletion

## Angelman Syndrome
Girl with inappropriate laughter + intellectual disability
Chromosome? 15
Paternal gene turned off → paternal uniparental disomy or maternal deletion

# Drugs That Improve Survival in HF
ACE-I
Spironolactone (AE: gynecomastia from androgen receptor antagonism) / eplerenone
Beta-blockers: Metoprolol, carvedilol, bisoprolol
Hydralazine / Isosorbide dinitrate (BiDil) **in African-Americans**

# Antipsychotic Side Effects
Dilated cardiomyopathy → clozapine
Hyperprolactinemia → risperidone OR aripiprazole
Torsades → ziprasidone (QT prolongation)
Increased Hgb A1c → olanzapine (obesity & metabolic syndrome)
Neutropenic fever → clozapine
Visual difficulty + lens opacification → quetiapine (cataracts)

# Paraneoplastic — LEMS
55 yo M smoker + chronic cough + hard time rising from seated positions → LEMS 2/2 small cell lung cancer
Pathophys? Ab to presynaptic voltage-gated Ca++ channel
Dx? EMG/nerve conduction study — Incremental pattern with repeated stimulation
Other paraneoplastic phenomena of SCLC: Ectopic ACTH (doesn't suppress with high-dose dex), SIADH

# Choriocarcinoma
#1 RF? Gestational trophoblastic dz (especially complete mole)
Arises from? Trophoblastic tissue
Marker? beta-hCG
Metastasizes to? Lungs
Tx? Methotrexate
Choriocarcinoma + A-fib + lid lag + hyperreflexia → hyperthyroidism
Pathophys? beta-hCG and TSH have similar structures → beta-hCG stimulates TSH receptors
Dx? Elevated T3/T4 + suppressed TSH
