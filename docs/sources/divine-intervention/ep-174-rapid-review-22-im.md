# CULTURE-NEGATIVE ENDOCARDITIS

Cattle farmer with suspected endocarditis, cultures negative → Coxiella endocarditis
Also consider HACEK organisms

# PELLAGRA (NIACIN DEFICIENCY)

Hx carcinoid syndrome + chronic diarrhea + forgetfulness + 20/30 MMSE + skin rash → pellagra 2/2 niacin deficiency
Pathophys: Small bowel or appendiceal tumor makes excess serotonin, drains tryptophan stores → niacin can't be produced

# EHLERS-DANLOS SYNDROME

Hx abdominal surgery + fevers + vomiting + no flatus + distention (SBO), loses consciousness & becomes hypotensive, imaging shows bowel contents throughout abdomen → small bowel rupture 2/2 Ehlers-Danlos
Pathophys: Collagen defect
Inheritance: AD
Associations: Aortic dissection, Aortic aneurysm, MVP, Intracranial aneurysms → SAH

# FOLATE DEFICIENCY IN HEMOLYTIC ANEMIA

Pt with hx hereditary spherocytosis + MCV 110 → folate deficiency
Run out of folate (B9) much faster than B12
Associations: Hx hemolytic anemia (burn through folate stores), Sickle cell disease, Thalassemias, Alcoholic (alcohol poisons enzymes that help absorb B12)
Prevention: Daily folate supplement

# ALPHA-1 ANTITRYPSIN DEFICIENCY

35 yo M smoked 0.5 ppd x 10 years + increased sputum + cough + CXR mild hyperinflation. Uncle died of liver problem → alpha-1 antitrypsin deficiency
Inheritance: Autosomal codominant
Pathophys: Alpha-1 antitrypsin is antiprotease that protects lung structural proteins from protease degradation
Lung manifestation: Panacinar emphysema
Liver manifestation: Cirrhosis

# MICROCYTIC ANEMIA IN 50+ → COLONOSCOPY

53 yo M with 30 pack-year hx + fatigue + Hgb 8 + MCV 65. NBS → colonoscopy
Microcytic anemia in 50+ yo = NBS colonoscopy

# DIVERTICULITIS

LLQ pain + fever → diverticulitis
Dx: CT scan with contrast
Colonoscopy 6 weeks after episode to r/o colon cancer (do NOT do when acutely ill)

# CHEMOTHERAPY SIDE EFFECTS

## Vinca Alkaloids (Vincristine, Vinblastine)
Lung cancer pt on chemo with pins & needles → peripheral neuropathy
Mechanism: Inhibit polymerization of microtubules
Other pharm causes of peripheral neuropathy: Taxanes (paclitaxel), Cisplatin

## Pulmonary Fibrosis
Pt on chemo for colon cancer + SOB + dry cough + CT with increased interstitial markings → pulmonary fibrosis
Drugs: Busulfan, Bleomycin (G2 phase, free radicals → strand breaks), Methotrexate (inhibits DHFR)
Restrictive pattern (FEV1/FVC normal or mildly increased)
If profound bone marrow suppression from methotrexate → NBS: Give leucovorin (folinic acid analog)

# TOXOPLASMOSIS IN HIV

HIV + HA & seizures + brain MRI with multiple ring-enhancing lesions → toxoplasmosis
Tx: Sulfadoxine/pyrimethamine (can also cause bone marrow suppression)

# LATEX ALLERGY

#1 RF: Healthcare worker or prolonged exposure to healthcare system
Type HSR: Type 1

# HYPERSENSITIVITY REACTIONS

## Type 1 (IgE mediated)
Pathophys: Antigen binding to IgE → cross linking on mast cell → degranulation
Examples: Anaphylaxis, Asthma, Latex allergy

## Type 2 (Antibody-mediated)
Pathophys: Antibodies against a particular antigen
Examples: Autoimmune hemolytic anemia

## Type 3 (Ab-Ag complex-mediated)
Pathophys: Antibody-antigen complexes accumulate & deposit
Examples: Vasculitis, Serum sickness, Most nephrotic/nephritis

## Type 4 (Delayed)
Examples: Contact dermatitis, Tuberculin skin testing
