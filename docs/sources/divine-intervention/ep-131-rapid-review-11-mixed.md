# ITP

25F with multiple nose bleeds, Plt 10k, WBC nl, Hgb nl: ITP
Pathophys: Ab-GpIIbIIIa
RF: lupus
Tx:
- Mild: observation
- Severe: steroids, splenectomy
*prior to splenectomy, vaccinate against SHiN

# NEONATAL FAILURE TO PASS MECONIUM

Newborn, has not pooped for a wk:
DDx:
- Hirschsprung's disease = aganglionic distal colon
- Meconium ileus 2/2 CF
- T. cruzi (big heart, big colon, big esophagus)
Supposed to poop within the first 48h!

| Dx | Arthrocentesis with high WBC | MRI, Triple phase bone scan |

# CONTRACEPTION

Postpartum, looking for means of contraception — what to avoid?
Estrogen-based contraceptives!

## Other contraindications for estrogen
- VTE, Stroke, MI
- Breast ca (ER/PR responsive, *also avoid progestin-containing OCP)
- Hepatic adenoma
- >35 + smoker
- HTN

## Copper IUD
Best means of emergency contraception
c/I: heavy menstrual bleeding

## Barrier methods
Protect against STIs? Condoms or abstinence
Spermicides do NOT offer protection!
RF: IUD, diaphragm → c/b toxic shock syndrome

## Injectable progestin analogs q3mo
c/I: rapid return to fertility

## Progestin IUD Mirena
--| adenomyosis

## Basal body temperature
LH surge → Progestin makes temperature rise = the egg is around! Have intercourse!

# ALPORT SYNDROME

Kiddo w difficulty hearing, cataracts, deaf uncles, RBC casts in urine: Alport syndrome
Sx: boy who "can't see, can't pee, can't hear a high C"
Pathophys: COL4A5 mutation @ Type4 collagen; X-linked

# GENITAL WARTS & PAP SMEAR

Pathophys: HPV 6, 11
NBS: screen for STIs, Pap smear if >21Y

## Pap smear schedule
- Cytology: q3y
- HPV + cytology: q5y
- *pap smear every year if immunodeficient
- HPV vaccine 11-26Y
- + pap smear → colposcopy
- Atypical glandular cells? Endometrial biopsy!
MCC death d/t cervical cancer: invasion of ureters

# ALZHEIMER'S DEMENTIA

75M with impaired IADL: Alzheimer's dementia
Pathophys: low Ach (@ basal nucleus of Meynert), decreased choline acetyltransferase (CHAT)
CHAT deficiency: no Ach! — edrophonium test, similar sx to MG
Vs. MG: + edrophonium test
RF: age, FMHx, Down syndrome

# RHEUMATOID ARTHRITIS

35F morning stiffness, pain in fingers: rheumatoid arthritis
Dx: anti-RF (IgM-IgG), anti-CCP
Tx: methotrexate, NSAIDs
*PFTs before starting methotrexate d/t AE: pulmonary fibrosis, hepatotoxicity
Rescue bone marrow with leucovorin

# PHEOCHROMOCYTOMA

Episodic palpitations, HA, hypertension: pheochromocytoma
NBS: check metanephrine levels (HVA, VMA)
Dx: MIBG scan, posterior mediastinum
Pathophys: chromaffin cells of adrenal medulla (~ sympathetic neuron)
RF: MEN2 (MCC sx is MTC), NF1
Tx:
1. Alpha blockade i.e. phentolamine, phenoxybenzamine
2. Beta blockade
3. Surgery

Profound hypertensive crisis during surgery d/t spilling catecholamines?
NBS: phentolamine, nitroprusside

# PERTUSSIS

Anti-vaxxer, coughing paroxysms: pertussis
Tx: macrolide
AE: prolonged QT interval, diarrhea

# IgG4 RELATED DISEASE

"Weird cluster of sx": rock-hard thyroid gland, hypothyroid, recurrent epigastric pain radiating to back, no biliary tract dz, no EtOH: IgG4 related disease
Manifestations:
- Riedel's thyroiditis
- Interstitial lung disease
- Autoimmune pancreatitis
- PSC
- Recurrent cholecystitis
- Prostatitis
- Urinary retention 2/2 retroperitoneal fibrosis
