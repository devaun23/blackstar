# DEPRESSION

"Down, depressed, hopeless, little interest over past two weeks?"
Sx: Depression, Sleep, Interest (loss), Guilt, Energy (less), Concentration, Appetite, Psychomotor, Suicidality

+ Mania = bipolar disorder → Tx: lithium (AE: nephrogenic DI → amiloride/triamterene, hypothyroid), SGA
+ varies with seasons = seasonal affective disorder → Tx: SSRIs + phototherapy
+ loss of a loved one, bereavement (for multiple days, horrible guilt) = MDD +/- psychosis >>> grief reaction (no tx)
+ assoc 1 week before menses = PMS → PMDD (Dx: symptom diary)

DDx: hypothyroid, stroke, Parkinson's, Cushing's, interferon (HepC), BB

## SSRI Selection
- CHF, CVD: sertraline
- Sexual dysfunction, smoking: bupropion (c/I bulimia)
- Weight loss: mirtazapine
- C/I pregnancy: paroxetine

## Treatment Duration
1st episode → SSRI for 6-9 mos → 2nd episode → SSRI for 12 months → 3rd episode → tx for life (or if 2nd episode comes within 1 year of 1st)

Serotonin syndrome: SSRI, MAOi, linezolid, dextromethorphan/cough syrup, tramadol, buspirone → myoclonus, hyper-reflexic
***ensure no history of mania

# ANXIETY

Sx: irritable, disturbances of sleep, worriers
C/b: psych co-morbidities
Tx: SSRI, SNRI, buspirone, CBT
If anxiety attack, flight, etc → one-time benzo

+ fear of poor performance in social settings = social anxiety disorder → Tx: propranolol (c/I asthma), benzo

## Panic Disorder
Panic attack = palpitations, sweating, SOB, losing control → Tx: one-time benzo
Multiple attacks → panic disorder → Tx: SSRI, CBT

## Somatic Symptom Disorder
+ somatic symptom which causes distress in life → Tx: same physician
DDx: malingering (+incentive-based), factitious (+primary gain), factitious by proxy (+imposed on another), conversion disorder (+non-localizable neuro)

## Illness Anxiety Disorder
+ worry about general health, preoccupation with health-related activities = illness anxiety disorder/hypochondriasis

# PTSD

Sx: re-experiencing traumatic events, avoidance of assoc stimulus, hyper-arousal w stimuli
Dx: screen for comorbid psych, abuse
Tx: SSRI, CBT, prazosin → flashbacks
NEVER pick benzos as tx for PTSD!

# OCD

Sx: obsession +/- compulsion
Obsession = intrusive idea, thought → anxiety
Compulsion = things that you do to relieve it
Tx: CBT exposure & response prevention, SSRI, clomipramine, olanzapine
DDx: OCPD (do not recognize as unreasonable)

# EATING DISORDERS

## Anorexia nervosa
BMI < 18.5; distorted body image, amenorrhea
c/b osteoporosis, arrhythmias, refeeding syndrome (MCC: hypophosphatemia)
Tx: CBT

## Bulimia
BMI > 18.5; compensatory behaviors (laxative, vomit)
Tx: CBT, fluoxetine, imipramine
DDx: binge eating disorder (no compensatory)
→ hypochloremic hypokalemic metabolic alkalosis

# SCHIZOPHRENIA

Sx: disorganized speech, illogical thoughts, disorganized behavior, catatonia, flat affect, alogia, avolition
Tx: SGA (olanzapine, risperidone, aripiprazole, quetiapine)

# ADHD

Sx: childhood onset, inattention +/- hyperactivity in 2 settings
RF: substance abuse, mood disorder
Tx: stimulant (methylphenidate), atomoxetine (SNRI)
Stimulants c/I CVD
