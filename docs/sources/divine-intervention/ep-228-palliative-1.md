# EPIDEMIOLOGY
#1 cause of death in US: Heart disease (incidence decreasing)
#2 cause of death: Cancer (trend increasing)
Most healthcare spending goes to chronic disease, especially last few years of life

Terminal illness = reasonable medical expectation of <6 months survival

# PRINCIPLES OF PALLIATIVE CARE
- Manage sxs
- Avoid invasive diagnostic testing & exams
- Goals of care discussions on a regular basis

Why don't physicians initiate goals of care discussions?
- Fear of bringing up death
- Time constraints

# COMMUNICATION — BREAKING BAD NEWS (PSPIKES)
P = Prepare for discussion
S = Setting
P = Patient's Perception (assessing understanding)
I = Information (how much would you like to know?)
K = Knowledge
E = Empathy & acknowledge emotions
S = Sharing next steps & summarize

# ADVANCED DIRECTIVES
Living will → patient completes while coherent, details specific wishes
Healthcare proxy / durable POA → designated person for healthcare decisions if patient cannot
