# HEAT-RELATED ILLNESS
Heatstroke (exertional): soldier/athlete + AMS + Temp 104F → Tx: ice water immersion
Heatstroke (non-exertional): anticholinergic toxicity, adrenergic drugs, diuretics → Tx: evaporative cooling (NOT ice water!)
Heat exhaustion: no AMS (differentiator)
Ice water immersion CONTRAINDICATED in anticholinergic-induced heatstroke!

# MALIGNANT HYPERTHERMIA
Surgical pt + increasing PCO2 + high fever + leukocytosis
AD mutation in ryanodine receptors; Trigger: succinylcholine
Tx: stop drug → dantrolene (ryanodine receptor antagonist/CCB) → manage hyperkalemia → manage rhabdomyolysis

## Hyperkalemia management
Calcium gluconate → Insulin + glucose → Sodium bicarb → Albuterol → Fluids + furosemide → Sodium polystyrene sulfonate (risk bowel necrosis)

# NMS VS SEROTONIN SYNDROME
## NMS
Dopamine receptor blocker exposure (metoclopramide, antipsychotics, PD pt off levodopa)
AMS + muscle rigidity + high BP + tachycardia + NO myoclonus + DECREASED DTR
Tx: 1. Dantrolene 2. Dopamine agonist 3. Benzo (last resort)

## Serotonin Syndrome
SSRIs, SNRIs, TCAs, MAO-I, Linezolid, sumatriptan, MDMA, St John's wort
Typically: switching serotonergic agents without 2-week washout
HAS myoclonus + INCREASED DTR (double positive)
Tx: 1. Benzo (over cyproheptadine) 2. Cyproheptadine

# HYPERTENSION SCENARIOS
## Aortic dissection (Marfan/Turner + sudden severe chest pain)
1st line: beta blocker (including labetalol); Hydralazine ALWAYS wrong

## CHF exacerbation + pulmonary edema
Nitroglycerin/nitroprusside (venodilator → pulls blood from lungs)

## Scleroderma renal crisis
ACE-I/ARB (steroids NEVER right for scleroderma)

## Tyramine crisis (MAO-I + restaurant food)
Tx: Phentolamine (reversible non-selective alpha blocker)

## Pregnancy hypertension
Labetalol, nicardipine, hydralazine; NEVER ACE-I/ARBs
