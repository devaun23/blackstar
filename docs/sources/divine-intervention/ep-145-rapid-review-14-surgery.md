# GI Surgery

## Meckel's Diverticulum

Kid w/ mild RLQ pain + guaiac positive stool → Meckel's diverticulum
Pathophys: failure of obliteration of vitelline duct (omphalomesenteric duct)
Dx? Technetium-99m pertechnetate scan
Gastric mucosa secretes acid → erodes GI tract → bleeding (only in symptomatic cases)
Tx? Resection

## Small Bowel Obstruction (Crohn's)

Pt w/ hx Crohn's + n/v + no bowel movements → SBO
Obstructed d/t strictures (GERD can also cause strictures)

## Toxic Megacolon

Pt w/ hx UC + severe abdominal pain, distended, transverse colon → toxic megacolon
NBS? Ex lap
Other causes: C. diff, Chagas
Screening colonoscopies: 8 yrs after UC diagnosis, then every 1-2 yrs
PSC + UC diagnosed same time → screening colonoscopy at time of diagnosis


# Thyroid Pathology

## Hashimoto's Thyroiditis

3 mos fatigue + low mood + bradycardic + carpal tunnel → Hashimoto's thyroiditis
Most common cause of hypothyroid in US: Hashimoto's w/ anti-TPO abs
Most common cause in newborn: thyroid dysgenesis
RAIU: minimal to none

## De Quervain's Thyroiditis

Painful, tender thyroid + hx recent viral URI + decreased radioactive uptake
Can also cause hyperthyroid in early thyrotoxic phase

## Other Causes of Hyperthyroidism

Factitious hyperthyroid → low TSH, low radioactive uptake, low thyroglobulin
Graves' → autoantibodies against TSH receptor, diffusely increased uptake on radioactive scan
Struma ovarii → elevated T3/T4, low TSH, decreased uptake on radioactive scan
Toxic adenoma → only 1 hotspot on radioactive scan
Multinodular goiter → multiple hotspots interspersed w/ cold spots

## Thyroid Cancer

Papillary thyroid cancer = most common ("popular")
Histology: psammoma bodies + Orphan Annie eye nuclei

Follicular thyroid cancer → spreads hematogenously

80 yo pt w/ profound wt loss + enlarging thyroid → anaplastic thyroid cancer

Multiple kidney stones + recurrent abdominal pain + enlarging thyroid → medullary thyroid cancer
Association: MEN2A (hypercalcemia)
Tumor marker: calcitonin → can cause prolonged QT
Histology: apple green birefringence on Congo red stain (amyloid deposition)

## Reidel's Thyroiditis

Rock hard thyroid gland → Reidel's thyroiditis (fibrosis of thyroid)
Associated w/ IgG4-related disease:
- Autoimmune pancreatitis (sausage-shaped pancreas)
- Retroperitoneal fibrosis (bilateral hydronephrosis)
- Autoimmune gallbladder disease

## Psammoma Bodies

Papillary thyroid cancer, meningiomas (parasagittal, dural tail), mesothelioma (ferruginous bodies)


# Volvulus

Coffee bean sign w/ head toward RUQ → sigmoid volvulus
Coffee bean sign w/ head toward LUQ → cecal volvulus


# Lung Cancer

Pt heavy smoker + wt loss + dullness to percussion + decreased breath sounds + large pleural effusion + coin lesion
NBS? Thoracentesis w/ cytology (malignant cells → stage 4 cancer)

## Adenocarcinoma

#1 cause of lung cancer

## Squamous Cell Carcinoma

Histology: keratin pearls
Paraneoplastic: hypercalcemia (PTHrP)

## Small Cell Lung Cancer

Neuroendocrine origin, already metastatic at diagnosis
Paraneoplastic syndromes:
- SIADH (hypoosmolar serum, hyperosmolar urine)
- Lambert-Eaton (ab against presynaptic Ca channels → proximal muscle weakness improves w/ use)
- Cushing's (ACTH does not suppress w/ dexamethasone)

## Lung Cancer Complications

SVC syndrome → NBS: radiation
Pancoast tumor → Horner's syndrome
Hypertrophic pulmonary osteoarthropathy → get imaging
Partial Horner's syndrome (ptosis + miosis) → cluster headache


# Neurology

## Subarachnoid Hemorrhage

Xanthochromia → SAH
Worst HA of pt's life, often mimics meningitis but is sudden onset
NBS? Non-contrast head CT → LP
Give nimodipine to prevent superimposed ischemic stroke

## Herpes Encephalitis

Hyperintense signaling in temporal regions on MRI
RBCs in CSF → cover w/ IV acyclovir (only give IV if admitting pt)

## Creutzfeldt-Jakob Disease

6 wk period of memory loss + myoclonus + proprioception loss → CJD
Elevated protein 14-3-3

## Narcolepsy

Low levels of orexin/hypocretin in CSF
Dx? Sleep study
Tx? Scheduled naps + stimulants (modafinil) + sodium oxybate (for cataplexy)
