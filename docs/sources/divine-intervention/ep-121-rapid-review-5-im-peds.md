# SPONTANEOUS BACTERIAL PERITONITIS

Chronic alcoholic, AMS, low-grade fever, vague abd pain w/ distension: spontaneous bacterial peritonitis
NBS: paracentesis (>250 WBC, bugs)
Tx: ceftriaxone, fluoroquinolone (gram+ & gram- coverage)
Ppx: fluoroquinolone
RF: ascites, peritoneal dialysis, VP shunt (h/o hydrocephalus)

# ACUTE INTERSTITIAL NEPHRITIS

Infection few days ago, got a drug, now 101.5 fever w/ small rash on face and trunk, eosinophilia: acute interstitial nephritis
Sx: fever + rash + eosinophils (in blood or urine)

## DDx eosinophilia
Drugs i.e. abx, Neoplasms i.e. hematologic malignancies, Asthma, Addison's disease, AIN, Collagen/vascular disease i.e. scleroderma, Parasites

Dx: urine with elevated eosinophils

## Urinalysis Findings

| Finding | Dx | Etiology |
|---|---|---|
| Eosinophils | AIN | Drug |
| WBC casts | Pyelonephritis | |
| Pigmented muddy brown casts | ATN | Intrarenal acute injury |

# BACK PAIN DIFFERENTIAL

## + lost sensation around buttocks, infertility → Leriche syndrome
Et: severe PAD @ distal aorta + iliac arteries
RF: smoking

## + lifting heavy boxes, sudden-onset severe back pain, paravertebral muscle tenderness → muscle strain
Tx: NSAIDs, activity modification

## + osteoporosis, positive straight leg raise → herniated disk
Et: nucleus pulposus herniation → impingement on nerve roots
Tx: rest + gradual return to activity for 6wks

## + sudden-onset, severe abdominal pain, BP 100/50 → ruptured AAA
Dx: "draped aorta sign" = calcifications in front of vertebral bodies
RF: smoking

# SPINAL MUSCULAR ATROPHY

6 mos kiddo with loss of motor milestones, fasciculations: spinal muscular atrophy
Vs. adult with fasciculations: ALS
Autosomal recessive @ chr5 SMN1 gene

# IMMUNODEFICIENCY SYNDROMES

## Bruton's agammaglobulinemia
Kiddo with multiple bouts of pneumococcal sepsis, giardia, family members with multiple severe infections
Et: X-linked mutation @ Bruton's tyrosine kinase (B-cell maturation)
"humoral immunity defect/antibody defect"
Vs. T cell → PCP, viral, fungal infections
Sx: show up after 6mos because mom's Ab protects until then
Dx: low levels of every Ig
Tx: monthly IVIG

## Wiskott-Aldrich syndrome
Kiddo with recurrent infections, petechiae on skin, bleeds when mom tries to brush teeth, eczema
Et: X-linked immunodeficiency
Sx: thrombocytopenia, eczema, recurrent infections

## Chronic Granulomatous Disease (CGD)
Kiddo with recurrent abscesses with bacteria
Et: mutation in NADPH oxidase (oxidative burst)
Sx: infection with catalase positive organisms (i.e. Staph, Serratia)

## DiGeorge syndrome
Kiddo with immunodeficiency, seizures, recurrent parainfluenza, prolonged QT interval
Et: failed development of 3rd/4th pouches (where T-cells mature)
T-cell deficiency → infections
Hypocalcemia → prolonged QT, seizures

DDx: infants of diabetic moms
Sx: hypoglycemia 2/2 hyperinsulinemia developed in utero, VSD, HOCM

# HYPONATREMIC SEIZURE

Pediatric seizure, on desmopressin for nocturnal enuresis: hyponatremic seizure 2/2 ADH-analog over-secretion
DDx volume depleted kiddo: replenish with isotonic saline
