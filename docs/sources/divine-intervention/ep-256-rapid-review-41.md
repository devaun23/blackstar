African kid + big jaw mass → Burkitt's lymphoma (t(8,14), starry sky pattern, EBV-linked)
EBV associations: Burkitt's, Hodgkin's (Reed-Sternberg), nasopharyngeal lymphoma, primary CNS lymphoma (HIV)
Barn worker + grains + weight loss + jaundice + liver mass → HCC (aflatoxins)
Joint pain + digital clubbing + lung cancer → hypertrophic pulmonary osteoarthropathy (esp adenocarcinoma)

## Small cell lung cancer paraneoplastic
SIADH (hyponatremia, hypertonic urine), ectopic ACTH (no suppression), LEMS (proximal weakness, incremental response)
Tx: chemo only
Squamous cell: cavitates (air-fluid levels), PTHrP → hypercalcemia
#1 cause of hypercalcemia in hospital = malignancy

## Tumor markers
+keratin = SCC; +chromogranin = endocrine (small cell); +GFAP = astrocytoma/GBM
+desmin = muscle (rhabdomyosarcoma); +vimentin = leiomyosarcoma; +S100 = neural crest (melanoma, Langerhans, schwannoma)
+AFP = yolk sac tumor, HCC; +b-hCG = choriocarcinoma; +PSA = prostate; +calcitonin = MTC
+CEA = colon, pancreatic; CA19-9 = pancreatic; CA125 = ovarian

## Ovarian tumors
Sertoli-Leydig: hyperandrogenism + adnexal mass (+testosterone)
Hyperandrogenism + normal testosterone → CT abdomen for adrenal tumor (DHEAS)
Granulosa cell: Call-Exner bodies, produce estrogen → precocious puberty in kids, endometrial cancer in postmenopausal

## Calcified masses DDx
Suprasellar in child + bitemporal hemianopsia → craniopharyngioma
Frontal lobe + fried egg → oligodendroglioma
Adnexal/anterior mediastinum → dermoid cyst/teratoma
Kid <1 yo crossing midline → neuroblastoma (posterior mediastinum)

## Alk phos elevation
Check GGT: elevated → liver/cholestatic; normal → bone (mets, osteoporosis, Paget's, placental in seminoma/pregnancy)

Struma ovarii: adnexal mass + hyperthyroid + AFib (most common arrhythmia in hyperthyroidism)
Neck mass + prolonged QT → hypocalcemia from calcitonin/MTC
