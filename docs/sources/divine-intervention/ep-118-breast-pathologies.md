# BREAST PATHOLOGY DIFFERENTIAL

## Mastitis
Unilateral erythema, tenderness, fever, recent postpartum

## Abscess
Unilateral painful, fluctuant mass, recent postpartum, fever

## Engorgement
Bilateral engorgement in recent postpartum F w/fever

## Galactocele
Unilateral could present as tender, fluctuant mass, in breast feeding F, NO FEVER

## Inflammatory Breast Cancer
Unilateral process in non-postpartum older F (50s+), peau d'orange
