Condyloma acuminatum: HPV 6,11; Tx: podophyllin

Legionella: dehumidifier + SOB + diarrhea + thrombocytopenia + hyponatremia → Dx: urine antigen; Tx: macrolides; silver stain
H. pylori: epigastric pain worse with meals; also silver stain
Thoracic outlet syndrome: weightlifter + numbness/weakness + weak radial/ulnar pulse
