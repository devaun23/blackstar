# ICU Nutrition
Sick ICU pt on vent → best nutrition: Enteral (NG tube)
Benefits: GI tract stimulation, avoid vascular line infection
TPN vitamin/mineral deficiency? Selenium deficiency

Wilson's disease on chelator (trientine/penicillamine) + dysgeusia + alopecia + poor wound healing → zinc deficiency

# Contraindications to Birth Control
Wilson's disease → No Cu IUD
Heavy menstrual bleeding → No Cu IUD
Forgets meds → No OCPs
Postpartum → No estrogen (VTEs & reduces milk)
Needs rapid return to fertility → No Depo-Provera
Hx osteoporosis → No Depo-Provera
Doesn't want weight gain → No Depo-Provera
Hx breast cancer → Cu IUD only (nothing with estrogen or progesterone)
Recent PID → No IUD

# Prosthetic Valve Endocarditis
Early (<60 days) → Staph epidermidis
Late (>60 days) → Staph aureus
>12 months → consider Strep viridans
Mechanical valve → anticoagulation for life (warfarin)

# Developmental Dysplasia of Hip
Clicky hip or leg length discrepancy in newborn
Dx: Ortolani & Barlow, US
Tx: Pavlik harness (>6 months: surgical reduction + spica cast)

# Muscular Dystrophies
## Duchenne
Gower's sign (hands to get up) + large calves
Inheritance: X-linked recessive, Gene: DMD, Protein: Dystrophin (absent)
MCC death: Dilated cardiomyopathy (die before 30)
Tx: High calorie diet, PT, steroids

## Becker
Same gene (DMD), abnormal but partially functional dystrophin
Later onset (10-15), can live to 50s

## Limb-Girdle (LGMD)
Similar to Duchenne/Becker but in a girl — AD or AR

# Chagas Disease (Trypanosoma cruzi)
South American pt with orthopnea + pitting edema → dilated cardiomyopathy
Complications: Achalasia → megaesophagus, Toxic megacolon (destroys Auerbach's plexus)
Tx: Benznidazole or nifurtimox
Other toxic megacolon causes: C. diff, UC, Scleroderma with bacterial overgrowth

# Lithium Toxicity
Hx bipolar + tremors + hypernatremia
AEs: Nephrogenic DI (Li uses ENaC → Tx: amiloride/triamterene), Hypothyroidism, Teratogen (Ebstein's anomaly), Tremors, Seizures
If seizures → Emergent dialysis

# Oxytocin-ADH Cross-Reactivity
Pregnant woman on Pitocin + hyponatremia → Oxytocin cross-reacts with V2 receptor → aquaporin insertion
ADH: supraoptic nucleus; Oxytocin: paraventricular nucleus

# Cortisol and Mineralocorticoid Activity
Cushing's labs can look like primary hyperaldosteronism (hypokalemia + metabolic alkalosis)
11-beta hydroxylase deficiency: 11-deoxycorticosterone buildup → mineralocorticoid activity → HTN + hypokalemia

# Pneumothorax vs Air Embolism
Recent vascular procedure + SOB + JVD + no breath sounds one side → tension pneumothorax → needle decompression then chest tube
Recent vascular procedure + SOB + normal lung exam → air embolism → LLD + Trendelenburg (traps air in RV apex)

# Down Syndrome
Two mechanisms: Maternal nondisjunction, Robertsonian translocation (balanced in parent, unbalanced in child)
Complications: Endocardial cushion defect, Duodenal atresia/Hirschsprung's/annular pancreas, ALL, Early-onset Alzheimer's
Quad screen: High beta-hCG & inhibin A, Low AFP & estriol ("HIgh for HCG & Inhibin")
Contrast Edward's (trisomy 18): everything low ("HE is low")
Elevated AFP: Neural tube defect, Abdominal wall defect (omphalocele/gastroschisis)

# Surgery Indications
Always surgery for: Paraesophageal hernia, Femoral hernia (female on NBME)

# Leprosy
Exotic animal exposure + loss of sensation on nose tip/fingertips
Affects cold body regions — Dx: Skin biopsy
Tx: Dapsone + rifampicin + clofazimine × 2 years ("DRC")
Animal: Armadillo
