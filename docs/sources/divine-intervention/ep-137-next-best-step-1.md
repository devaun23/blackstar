# Neurology

55 yo M with unilateral vision loss, sxs resolved within 5 mins → TIA
NBSDx? Carotid ultrasound
NBSM? Antiplatelet agent (aspirin, clopidogrel) — NOT anticoagulant unless obviously has A-fib

55 yo M with WHOL, started 30 mins ago + nuchal rigidity + fever 100.9F → SAH
NBSDx? NCCT → If negative, do LP → xanthochromia
NBSM? Labetalol (control BP), Nimodipine (prevent ischemic stroke)

## Temporal Arteritis
50 yo F with unilateral HA + pain with chewing 2 weeks ago
NBS? High-dose corticosteroids → Temporal artery biopsy later

## Polymyalgia Rheumatica
Proximal shoulder pain & stiffness
NBS? Low-dose corticosteroids → Temporal artery biopsy later

## Meningitis
60 yo M w/ AMS for past 24 hrs + temp 102F + nuchal rigidity
If signs of elevated ICP → give abx
Otherwise, obtain LP then immediately give abx
Neonate or over 50 → ceftriaxone + vancomycin + ampicillin
Otherwise → ceftriaxone + vancomycin

## IIH
35 yo F with BMI 40 with morning HAs + blurry vision for last 3 weeks
NBS? LP
Tx: Acetazolamide, serial therapeutic LPs
Stop offending meds: Vit A analogs, Doxycycline

## Migraines
35 yo M with daily pulsatile headaches for 3 months, better in dark room
NBS? Chronic migraine medication: Beta blockers, Topiramate (AE: nephrolithiasis), TCA (toxicity: wide QRS)
Acute: Sumatriptan, Ergo derivative

## Cluster Headaches
35 yo M with severe unilateral headache + rhinorrhea + conjunctival injection
NBS? 100% O2

## Alzheimer's Disease
75 yo M forgets to turn off stove, forgets longtime friends
NBS? Brain MRI
Tx: AChE inhibitor (Donepezil, Galantamine, Rivastigmine)

# Rheumatology / MSK

Red, hot, swollen, painful knee + temp 102F → possible septic joint
NBS? Arthrocentesis
<2k WBC → OA
2-50k → RA
>50k → septic arthritis or crystalline arthropathy
Yellow needle-shaped, negatively birefringent → gout
Blue rhomboid, positively birefringent → CPPD

## Hypertrophic Pulmonary Osteopathy
75 yo M lifelong smoker + 6 weeks joint pain (fingers, legs) not improved with NSAIDs + finger clubbing
NBS? CXR

## Restless Leg Syndrome
57 yo F with insomnia due to "crawling sensation in leg"
NBS? Dopamine agonist (pramipexole, ropinirole) or primidone
Association? IDA

# Pulmonology

Lifelong smoker + 40 lbs weight loss + nodule in LUL + large L-sided pleural effusion → lung cancer
NBS? Thoracentesis
Malignant cells in pleural fluid → stage 4 → not surgical candidate

## COPD Exacerbation
Lifelong smoker + 7 days SOB + increased sputum production
NBS? IV corticosteroids + bronchodilators (Duoneb) + azithromycin (anti-inflammatory properties)

# GI

## SBP
Alcoholic M with AMS + spider angiomas/gynecomastia + asterixis + fever + mild diffuse abdominal tenderness + shifting dullness & fluid wave
NBS? Paracentesis → >250 PMNs = SBP
Tx? Ceftriaxone (+ FQ prophylaxis after recovered)
Associations: Peritoneal dialysis, VP shunts, Budd Chiari syndrome

## Alcoholic Pancreatitis
35 yo M alcoholic + severe mid abdominal pain radiates to back
NBS? IVF & NPO
Dx? Lipase (more specific than amylase)

## Cholecystitis
45 yo F with BMI 40 + severe RUQ pain + temp 101F + WBC 12k
NBS? RUQUS → If equivocal → HIDA scan ("Non-visualization of GB after 4 hrs" = positive)
Tx? Cholecystectomy

## Acalculous Cholecystitis
Severely ill hospitalized pt + RUQ pain + fever + leukocytosis + GB wall thickening but NO stones
NBSM? Percutaneous cholecystostomy — Do NOT perform cholecystectomy (high mortality!)

## Toxic Megacolon
33 yo M with BMI 18 + severe bloody diarrhea + distention + rebound/guarding → toxic megacolon 2/2 UC
NBS? Ex-lap
Ex-lap is NBSM for: Toxic megacolon (C. diff, UC, Chagas), Penetrating abdominal trauma, Free air under diaphragm

# Vascular

## Acute Limb Ischemia
50 yo M with MI 3 days ago or hx A-fib + severe sudden onset leg pain + cold mottled leg
NBS? 1st: IV heparin, 2nd: Vascular surgery consultation

## Acute Mesenteric Ischemia
50 yo M with MI 3 days ago or hx A-fib + severe abdominal pain
NBS? Angiography — Most likely vessel? SMA

## Peripheral Arterial Disease
Lifelong smoker with severe bilateral leg pain
NBS? ABI
< 0.9 → PAD, 0.9-1.0 → borderline, 1-1.4 → normal, > 1.4 → Monckeberg medial calcific sclerosis
NBS for ABI > 1.4? Toe-brachial index
Tx: 1st Supervised walking program, 2nd Cilostazol (PDE inhibitor), 3rd Bypass (arteriography first!)
PAD causes: painful ulcers on tips of digits + cool pale skin with dermal atrophy + decreased pulses

# Psychiatry

## Schizophrenia
Pt with hx hearing voices + multiple hospitalizations + stops taking meds
NBSIM? Monthly haloperidol decanoate injections

## Antipsychotic Side Effects
Acute dystonia (head "stuck") → diphenhydramine or benztropine
Akathisia (restless, pacing) → 1st: beta blocker, 2nd: benzodiazepine
Parkinsonian (resting tremor + bradykinesia) → 1st: benztropine/trihexyphenidyl, 2nd: dopamine agonists
Tardive dyskinesia (weird tongue movements) → Stop drug, switch to atypical antipsychotic

## Agranulocytosis from Clozapine
30 yo M tx-resistant schizophrenia + fever 103F + WBC 2k with 20% neutrophils → neutropenic fever 2/2 agranulocytosis 2/2 clozapine
NBS? Stop clozapine
Tx: Antipseudomonal agent (meropenem, imipenem)
Similar to pt on chemo or Graves' on methimazole

## GAD
30 yo M with 6 months diffuse worries → Consider SSRI or buspirone (partial serotonin receptor agonist)

## Depression + Sexual Side Effects
25 yo F on SSRI/SNRI wants to stop due to sexual side effects
NBS? Switch to bupropion (no sexual side effects)
Other indications: smoking cessation, weight loss
CI: anorexia/bulimia, hx seizures

## Pseudodepression
50 yo F + guilt + loss of interest + hypersomnia + low energy + HR 49 → hypothyroidism
NBS? TSH

# Endocrinology

## Thyroid Storm
45 yo M + palpitations + 10 lb weight loss + BP 180/90 + HR 190, then somnolent
NBS? Propranolol then PTU then Lugol's solution (supersaturated KI)

## OSA/OHS
17 yo M BMI 40 + morning HAs + RR 8 + PCO2 60
NBS? Polysomnogram

# Cardiology

## CHF Exacerbation
60 yo M with hx AR + 3 days profound SOB + orthopnea + S3 heart sound
NBS? IV furosemide — Can also give nitrates for dyspnea 2/2 pulmonary edema
Terminal cancer with dyspnea → NBS: Morphine

## A-Fib with RVR
40 yo F with Graves' + palpitations + HR 220 + irregular, confused, BP drops 70/40 → HDUS
NBS? Synchronized cardioversion (DC cardioversion)
If HDS: Beta blocker, CCB (verapamil — associated with hyperprolactinemia), diltiazem
Rate control: beta blocker, non-dihydropyridine CCB, digoxin
Rhythm control: amiodarone

## A-Fib + WPW
25 yo M + palpitations + HR 250 + irregularly irregular + hx WPW
NBS? Procainamide
Do NOT use AV nodal blocking agents (BB, CCB, digoxin, adenosine) → enhances accessory tract → V-fib likely

# OB/GYN

## Adenomyosis
30 yo F + 3 months severe abdominal pain + globular boggy uterus → Hormonal IUD
If doesn't want more children → Hysterectomy
Dx: Uterine MRI — Gold standard: Diagnostic laparoscopy

## Amenorrhea / Oligomenorrhea
37 yo F + 3 months heavy menstrual bleeding + BMI 35 + menses every 70 days
NBS? OCPs (Endometrial biopsy may also be right due to high risk of endometrial hyperplasia/carcinoma)

## Rhogam
22 yo G2P1 F at 18 weeks undergoing amniocentesis
NBS? Give Rhogam

## Syphilis
25 yo M + painless lesion with heaped up borders on penis
NBS? RPR or VDRL → then treponemal test (FTA-Abs)
Tx: Penicillin G

# Infectious Disease

## Hep B Needle Stick
Nurse stuck by needle from Hep B positive pt, had vaccine 2 years ago with confirmed immunity → No further action
If non-immune → Hep B vaccine + Hep B immune globulin

## HIV Vaccinations
HIV pt on HAART, CD4 = 600
NBS: Influenza vaccine in fall, Pneumococcal vaccine (adults <65 if smoker/CKD/DM/immunocompromised; all adults >65)

# Anesthesia

## Malignant Hyperthermia
35 yo M going for eye surgery, 20 minutes after induction → stiffness + fever 105F
Pathophys: Abnormal ryanodine receptors → tons of Ca++ released from SR → muscle contraction
NBS? Dantrolene (blocks ryanodine receptor)
Inheritance: AD — Gene: Ryanodine receptor gene
