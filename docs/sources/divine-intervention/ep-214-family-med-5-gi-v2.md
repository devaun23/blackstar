# DIARRHEA DIFFERENTIAL

+ Bloody, RLQ pain "pseudo-appendicitis": Yersinia enterocolitica (Tx: supportive, >1 wk → abx)
+ Chronic watery, AIDS: cryptosporidium "acid-fast oocysts"
+ Recent abx, nursing home, hospitalization: C. diff
+ Hiking, IgA deficiency, chronic: Giardia lamblia (Dx: stool antigen; Tx: metronidazole)
+ Bloody, liver abscess: Entamoeba histolytica (Tx: metronidazole)
EHEC O157:H7: NO ABX (increased risk of HUS); loperamide generally c/I

## Osmotic gap = 290 - 2(stool Na + K)
Low osmotic gap <50: secretory diarrhea
High osmotic gap >100: osmotic diarrhea (improves with fasting)

+ Flushing sx: carcinoid syndrome (Dx: 5-HIAA)
+ Low BMI, self-conscious: laxative abuse (Dx: stool osmolarity <250)
+ Black colon: melanosis coli (no tx needed)
+ Recurrent pneumonia, sinusitis, Giardial diarrhea: Selective IgA deficiency (DDx: CVID)
+ Pancreatic insufficiency, diarrhea from birth: CF
+ Sugar-free candies, bloating: artificial sweetener-diarrhea
+ Bloating, w/o alarm sx: lactase deficiency
+ Bloating, better with bowel movement: IBS (Tx: TCA, alosetron — AE: ischemic colitis, loperamide, rifaximin)
+ 45-60Y, chronic, no temporal assoc, normal scope: microscopic colitis (Tx: oral budesonide)

# CELIAC DISEASE
Fat malabsorption, microcytic anemia, dermatitis herpetiformis (extensors), osteoporosis
Dx: 1. anti-TTG 2. EGD w/ biopsy (also anti-gliadin, anti-endomysial)
Tx: gluten-free diet, dapsone for dermatitis
Assoc: Hashimoto's, T1DM, IgA deficiency, small-bowel lymphoma

# CHRONIC PANCREATITIS
EtOH, hyperglycemia; Dx: stool fat, white streaks on imaging; Tx: pancreatic enzyme replacement

# BACTERIAL OVERGROWTH
Resected bowel, scleroderma, diabetic gastroparesis, macrocytic anemia
Et: bacteria love B12 and folate; Dx varies; Tx: abx

# WHIPPLE'S DISEASE
Middle aged man w joint pain, uveitis, neuro sx, cardiac sx
Dx: biopsy of small intestine; Tx: abx for 1 year

# TROPICAL SPRUE
International travel, chronic diarrhea, weight loss, fatty stools; Tx: bactrim, tetracycline, folate

# IBD
## Ulcerative Colitis
Chronic, better with smoking; Tx: proctocolectomy (starts at rectum)
Assoc: PSC (high AP/GGT)

## Crohn's Disease
Watery, abd pain, chronic fevers, fistulas
c/b: seronegative spondyloarthropathy, uveitis, erythema nodosum, pyoderma gangrenosum
Dx: colonoscopy (NEVER barium swallow → triggers toxic megacolon)
Tx acute: steroids; Tx chronic: ASA derivatives/TNF inhibitors, PCV23
6-MP and azathioprine inhibited by xanthine oxidase/allopurinol — Check TPMT!
